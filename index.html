Core Academic Research & Thesis Formulations

Post-Graduate Thesis Focus

A study on the effect of elastic-plastic material properties of metals on hardness values using finite element analysis

Investigated the computational mapping of elastic-plastic parameters across distinct metallic elements. Validated structural variations between standard mathematical representations and experimental macro-indentation testing regimes using high-density finite element model loops for optimization.

Thermal Systems Framework

Study of internal combustion engines & their components

Conducted structural analysis of kinematic and thermodynamic bounds dictating modern IC configurations. Developed a systematic comparative operational performance taxonomy isolating friction, cooling, and displacement metrics between two-stroke and four-stroke mechanical architectures.

Fluid Kinematics Prototype

Hydraulic Robotic Arm for pick and place operation

Engineered and built an operational multi-axis robotic apparatus operating on advanced fluid force transmission parameters. Implemented structural designs verifying fluid pressure distribution profiles in enclosed mechanical paths operating under rigorous hydrostatic constraints dictated by Pascal's Law.
