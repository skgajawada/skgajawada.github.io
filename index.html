About Me

Biography: I am an ambitious mechanical engineer and computational researcher dedicated to advancing computational modeling, simulation methodologies, and data-driven physical paradigms.

Research Vision: To bridge the gap between heavy structural dynamics modeling and cutting-edge machine learning. By combining structural dynamics, FEM, and deep neural architectures, I hope to pave the way for predictive aeroelastic designs and real-time control techniques.

Current Research: Investigating aeroelasticity, localized structural vibration controls, and smart composite integration schemes inside the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad.

Future Goals: I aim to establish industry-leading optimization frameworks for dynamic aerodynamic profiles and contribute actively to international scientific boards and interdisciplinary mechanical systems research.
