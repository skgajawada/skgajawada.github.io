About Me

Location: Ranga Reddy, Telangana, India

Department: Mechanical Engineering

Institution: IIT (ISM) Dhanbad

I am an active PhD Scholar specializing in engineering design within the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad. Over my professional trajectory, I have balanced academic exploration with over 7 years of higher education teaching experience as an Assistant Professor and Lecturer.

My domain expertise spans structural dynamics, composite shell modeling, and sophisticated finite element formulations. Driven by continuous technical learning, I map traditional analytical structural models onto modern data sciences, leveraging predictive platforms like Python, MATLAB, and specialized cloud systems like Dataiku to innovate across aerospace systems.

To advance mechanical and aerospace engineering through comprehensive computational research in aeroelasticity, localized structural flutter boundaries, and control strategies.

"My research intent is focused on integrating computational finite element modeling, fluid-structure interaction metrics, and data-driven artificial intelligence environments to pioneer design solutions for modern high-performance multi-physics platforms."

Believer in community development and direct personal metrics. I prioritize localized social responsibilities alongside research work:

  • Nasha Mukt Bharat Framework: Active pledge participant under the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India initiative, advocating a sustainable lifestyle.
Domains

Research Architecture

Aeroelasticity & Flutter

Evaluating unstable structural fluctuations and aerodynamic interactions across thin plates and aerospace wing sections using dynamic coupling matrix formulations.

Finite Element Methods (FEM)

Formulating customized high-fidelity elements, non-linear deformation models, and mesh criteria to analyze structural stress fields and structural deviations.

Structural Dynamics

Investigating structural modal variations, dynamic frequencies, damping performance indices, and transient harmonic patterns across structural geometries.

Composite Structures

Modeling multi-layered composite laminates, sandwich structure properties, and directional fiber distributions to enhance lightweight resilience metrics.

Machine Learning Integration

Deploying advanced predictive models, neural system surrogates, and regression frameworks to accelerate optimization of complex mechanical profiles.

Control System Analytics

Designing optimal closed-loop control schemes, active damping configurations, and PID control elements to mitigate unexpected structural fluctuations.
