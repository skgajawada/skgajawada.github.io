About Me

Biography

Highly motivated researcher dedicated to advancing mechanical and aerospace engineering systems. Currently pursuing a doctoral pathway at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, specializing in integrating robust computational physics modeling with contemporary data-driven architectures.

To blend advanced multi-physics formulation metrics with predictive intelligence platforms to construct modern, highly resilient, light flight envelopes capable of mitigating adverse dynamic non-linear fluid-structure instabilities.

Investigating aeroelastic limits, advanced composite layering properties, and active control loop configurations utilizing multi-domain structural models alongside deep mathematical predictive pipelines.

Active pledge participant within the national framework "Nasha Mukt Bharat, Khushaal Bharat" and dedicated multi-time voluntary blood donor (10+ milestones achieved) promoting holistic social welfare.

Research Core Domains

Aeroelasticity

Focuses on analyzing aerodynamic forces interaction on flexible structures, optimizing flutter margins and structural balance limits within high velocity environments.

Structural Dynamics

Modeling structural vibration patterns, evaluation of transient responses, frequency characterizations, and passive/active damping configurations.

Composite Structures

Evaluating multi-layered anisotropic properties to design exceptional stiffness-to-weight index components optimized for aerospace mechanical frameworks.

Finite Element Methods

Utilizing high-fidelity discretization algorithms to approximate stress fields, non-linearities, and multi-domain structural mechanics profiles.

Machine Learning

Fusing classical mechanics frameworks with artificial neural systems, system identification networks, and surrogate mathematical models.

MATLAB & Simulink

Programming robust custom numerical algorithms, block-diagram dynamic tracking loops, and algorithmic solvers for nonlinear evaluations.
