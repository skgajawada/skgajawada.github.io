About Me

Biography & Research Vision

I am currently a PhD scholar in the Design stream within the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad. Before dedicating myself back to full-time rigorous academic inquiry, I accumulated years of valuable educational experience working as an Assistant Professor and Lecturer, helping modern engineering students frame robust foundational concepts.

My primary technical objective is to advance structural safety and isolation mechanics across both aerospace and general mechanical engineering industries. By leveraging hybrid methodologies that merge experimental principles with precision modeling, my research aids in addressing critical modern operational failure dynamics.

Current Focus

Broadly encompassing mathematical modeling, finite element calculations, aerodynamic interactions, and deep model analysis, I work on integrating machine learning computational toolsets alongside classic control loop designs within MATLAB & Simulink to build scalable, optimized engineering components.

Research Areas

Aeroelasticity

Investigating severe interactions between aerodynamic lifting surfaces, structural mass distributions, and linear/nonlinear elastic boundary transformations.

Structural Dynamics

Analyzing natural frequencies, vibration responses under variable load spectra, phase transitions, and active computational dampener loops.

Composite Structures

Developing numerical calculation layer arrays and optimization schemes for variable multi-layer configurations under dynamic loading contexts.

Finite Element Analysis

Simulating intricate components under high-stress fields, plastic-deformation limits, multi-axial mechanical strain, and transient thermal grids.

Machine Learning

Deploying robust regression models, predictive analytics pipelines, and neural networks to accurately forecast material degradation profiles.

MATLAB & Simulink

Writing custom mathematical scripts, executing block control parameters, and simulating continuous dynamics behavior blocks.
