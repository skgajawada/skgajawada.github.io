About

I am a Research Scholar in Mechanical Engineering (Machine Design) at the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad. My research primarily focuses on Machine Design, Finite Element Analysis, Structural Dynamics, Vibration Analysis, Flutter Behaviour and Control, and Aerospace Engineering applications. I am particularly interested in understanding the dynamic behaviour of engineering systems and developing computational methodologies for advanced mechanical and aerospace structures. My work integrates traditional mechanical engineering principles with modern computational techniques, including numerical simulation, optimization, machine learning, computational intelligence, and digital twin technologies. Through this interdisciplinary approach, I aim to develop robust, efficient, and intelligent engineering solutions for next-generation mechanical and aerospace systems. With more than seven years of teaching and academic experience as an Assistant Professor, I have taught a wide range of undergraduate engineering subjects while actively participating in research, faculty development programs, workshops, and industry-academia initiatives. My long-term goal is to contribute to the advancement of machine design, computational mechanics, and data-driven engineering through high-impact research and innovation..
