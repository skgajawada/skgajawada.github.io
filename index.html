About Me

I am a Research Scholar in Mechanical Engineering (Machine Design) at the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad. My research primarily focuses on structural dynamics, aeroelastic flutter behaviour and control, and composite material applications. I aim to merge classic physical engineering models with cutting-edge data science techniques like predictive machine learning and digital twins.

With over seven years of professional experience as an Assistant Professor, I have a deep passion for both advanced mechanical systems research and mentoring the next generation of engineers. My teaching spans complex analytical mechanics, solids, and high-performance computing methodologies.

Academic Projects

Elastic-Plastic Metal Behavior in FEA

Finite Element Analysis

Investigated how the elastic-plastic material properties of various metals alter experimental hardness values relative to theoretical baselines, using advanced numerical design models in FEA to optimize predicted values.

Hydraulic Pick-and-Place Robotic Arm

Fluid Power Systems

Designed and built a functional hydraulic-actuated mechanical robot arm utilizing Pascal's Law to explore precision fluid transmission, load calculations, and mechanical kinematic linkages.

Internal Combustion Engine Diagnostics

Thermal Engineering

A fundamental study tracking operations, geometric structures, and dynamic imbalances of key IC Engine components, evaluating thermodynamic efficiencies across 2-stroke and 4-stroke cycles.
