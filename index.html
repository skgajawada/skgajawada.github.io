About Me

Biography & Research Vision

I am currently a PhD scholar in the Design stream of the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad. Prior to returning to academic research, I accumulated years of educational experience, working as an Assistant Professor and Lecturer, contributing to modern structural design engineering programs.

My primary objective is to advance aerospace and mechanical engineering industries through precise vibration isolation, structural dynamic assessments, and data-driven computational methodologies.

Current Focus

Broadly encompassing mathematical modeling, finite element calculations, aerodynamic interactions, and deep model analysis, I integrate machine learning tools with control designs in MATLAB & Simulink to build structural optimizations.

Research Areas

Aeroelasticity

Investigating interactions between aerodynamic loads, inertial forces, and elastic responses in mechanical assemblies.

Structural Dynamics

Analyzing natural frequencies, vibration behavior under varying load spectra, and complex damping integrations.

Composite Structures

Developing numerical computations and laminate design layups under severe structural dynamics constraints.

Finite Element Analysis

Simulating complex structures under static, non-linear elastic-plastic, thermal, and mechanical loading profiles.

Machine Learning

Deploying predictive models, federated structures, and neural systems to estimate failure modes and design parameters.

MATLAB & Simulink

Developing numeric scripts, control loops, and optimization pipelines across critical design layers.
