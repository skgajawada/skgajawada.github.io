About Me

Background & Academic Vision

I am an passionate academician and structural engineer currently executing advanced doctoral-level research in Machine Design within the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), Dhanbad.

Throughout my academic tenure, I have merged classical mechanics alongside computational, data-driven pathways (Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Big Data Engine Studios) to design high-impact engineering resolutions.

Research Vision

To integrate continuous computational frameworks, experimental dynamics modeling, and data-driven methods to develop next-generation aerospace and automotive composite laminates capable of withstanding dynamic vibrations and severe flutter margins.

Engineering Accomplishments

Research Projects

Finite Element Analysis

Elastic-Plastic Metal Properties Formulation

Investigated the exact structural behavior of elastic-plastic properties on metal blocks under concentrated high-load zones, calculating variance curves away from traditional metrics.

Objective: Map hardness variance via finite element calculations from theoretical to experimental setups.
Technologies: ANSYS, SolidWorks, MATLAB.
Thermal Engineering

IC Engine Performance Configuration

Constructed configurations evaluating operating mechanics differences between standard 2-stroke and 4-stroke thermodynamic engine dynamics under stress.

Objective: Introduce thermodynamic fundamentals & structural performance variations.
Technologies: Thermodynamics, Mechanical Layout Modeling.
Robotics & Fluid Control

Hydraulic Robotic Arm for Pick & Place Operation

Constructed a scale fluid-pressure powered mechanics rig, establishing robust force-transfer properties mapping Pascal's structural dynamics equations to achieve high positioning accuracy.

Objective: Design zero-loss fluid transmission configurations applying Pascal's Law.
Technologies: Hydraulics Fluid CAD, Hydro-mechanical simulation modeling.
