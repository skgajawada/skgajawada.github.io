About Me

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Mechanical Engineering (Design) at the Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad. My research focuses on computational mechanics and intelligent engineering systems, particularly in Machine Design, Finite Element Analysis, Structural Dynamics, Aeroelasticity, Flutter Analysis and Control, Composite Structures, and Machine Learning for Engineering Applications.

Prior to joining IIT (ISM), I served as an Assistant Professor for more than seven years, teaching undergraduate mechanical engineering courses while actively participating in research, faculty development programmes, technical workshops, and industry-academia collaborations.
