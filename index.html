About Me

Research Vision

To integrate advanced dynamic computing, FEM simulation and ML methods to design smart, stable mechanical solutions for structural problems.

Socio-Responsibility

Committed pledge-taker for drug-free communities (Nasha Mukt Bharat) and a proud 10-time active voluntary blood donor.

Biography

I am a dedicated PhD Researcher in Mechanical Design at the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad. Over my seven-year tenure as an educator and researcher, I have focused heavily on advanced computational structural methods, structural mechanics, and materials engineering.

Current Focus & Direction

My academic interests focus on structural safety analysis, finite element structural methods (FEM), and aeroelasticity. By matching theoretical mechanics with high-fidelity MATLAB and Simulink controls, I work on structural modeling, and prediction protocols utilizing data-driven Machine Learning techniques.

Research Focus Areas

Aeroelasticity

Study of interactions between aerodynamic, elastic and inertial forces in structures, focusing on dynamic instability and flutter limits.

Finite Element Analysis

Implementation of robust 1D, 2D and 3D FE algorithms to solve static, dynamic, linear and nonlinear engineering challenges.

Structural Dynamics

Modal, harmonic, transient and random vibrations modeling to analyze response configurations under varying dynamic excitation cycles.

Composite Structures

Structural optimization and stress performance analysis of layered anisotropic and composite laminate components.

Machine Learning

Integrating surrogate models, regression, and deep network algorithms for physics-informed predictive design diagnostics.

MATLAB & Simulink

Comprehensive system design, control optimization loops, numerical solver scripting, and complex signal generation.
