About Me

Researcher • Educator • Engineer

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Mechanical Engineering (Design) at the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad. My research focuses on understanding the dynamic behaviour of engineering structures using computational methods, numerical simulations and artificial intelligence.

With more than seven years of teaching experience, I have taught undergraduate and postgraduate mechanical engineering courses while continuously engaging in research, curriculum development and professional training.
