About Me

Background & Academic Vision

I am an passionate academician and structural engineer executing advanced doctoral research in Machine Design within the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the **Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), Dhanbad**.

Throughout my academic tenure, I have merged classical mechanical design parameters with modern computation, data-driven systems, and automation platforms to scale structural and system integrity.

Research Vision

Integrating computational frameworks, experimental dynamics, and artificial intelligence models to configure high-performance composite structures and vibration damping applications.

Engineering Accomplishments

Research Projects

Finite Element Analysis

Elastic-Plastic Metal Properties Formulation

Investigated the exact structural behavior of elastic-plastic properties on metal blocks under concentrated high-load zones, calculating variance curves away from traditional metrics.

Objective: Map hardness variance via finite element calculations.
Technologies: ANSYS, SolidWorks, MATLAB.
Thermal Engineering

IC Engine Performance Configuration

Constructed instructional configurations evaluating differences between standard 2-stroke and 4-stroke thermodynamic engine dynamics under stress.

Objective: Improve thermal load calculations.
Technologies: Thermodynamics Modeling, MATLAB.
Robotics & Fluid Control

Fluidic Hydro-Mechanical Robotic Arm

Constructed a scale fluid-pressure powered mechanics rig, establishing robust force-transfer properties mapping Pascal's structural dynamics equations.

Objective: Design zero-loss hydrostatic configurations.
Technologies: Hydraulics Fluid CAD, Pascal Dynamics modeling.
