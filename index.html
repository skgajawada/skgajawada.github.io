Research Thrusts

Aeroelasticity

Formulating structural boundaries to model instabilities (such as structural divergence and critical flutter speeds) on thin plates subjected to high-velocity aerodynamic regimes.

Structural Dynamics

Solving complex multi-degree-of-freedom equations of motion, mathematical analysis of natural frequencies, vibrational modes, and isolating dynamic transient loadings.

Composite Materials

Optimizing stacking sequences of orthotropic fiber-reinforced plates to maximize stiffness parameters, load paths, and stability constraints in high-performance structures.

Finite Element Methods (FEM)

Writing custom solvers, building mesh-independent algorithms, and evaluating stress-strain fields across dynamic interfaces in solid structures.
