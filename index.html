About Me

Hello! I'm Gajavada Sanjeevkumar

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Machine Design at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad. My research interests include Aeroelasticity, Composite Structures, Structural Dynamics, Finite Element Analysis, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, MATLAB, and Active Vibration Control. Previously, I worked as an Assistant Professor for more than seven years, teaching core Mechanical Engineering subjects.

Research Interests

My research integrates classical mechanical engineering with modern computational intelligence to solve challenging structural and aeroelastic problems.

Aeroelasticity

Flutter analysis, aeroelastic modeling, active flutter suppression, wing dynamics and stability.

Composite Structures

Hybrid laminates, sandwich plates, vibration behaviour and failure analysis.

Finite Element Analysis

Plate and shell elements, structural modeling, modal analysis and numerical simulation.

Structural Dynamics

Free vibration, forced vibration, damping, eigenvalue analysis and modal reduction.

Machine Learning

Artificial Neural Networks, Federated Learning, Data Science and intelligent engineering systems.

Control Systems

LQR control, active vibration control, nonlinear dynamic systems and MATLAB/Simulink.
