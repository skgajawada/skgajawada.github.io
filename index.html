Career Objective & Vision

To advance mechanical and aerospace engineering systems through continuous cutting-edge research in aeroelasticity, structural dynamics, finite element methods, composite structures, and control mechanics. I aim to merge rigorous analytical and computational formulation models with modern data-driven computational pathways, including Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), to solve highly non-linear, multi-physics structural challenges.

Driving innovations across advanced aerospace engineering ecosystems. Designing reliable, lightweight flight profiles and complex structures capable of managing unstable aeroelastic fluctuations and severe dynamic loads using advanced composite layers and hybrid algorithms.

Currently deep into doctoral exploration at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad. Concurrently combining predictive intelligence models alongside active structural systems using analytical software environments such as MATLAB, Simulink, and high-fidelity FEA platforms.

Believer in social balance and dynamic community impact. Recognized as a dedicated multi-time blood donor volunteer (10+ donation campaigns achieved) and an active pledge participant within the national initiative "Nasha Mukt Bharat, Khushaal Bharat" framework managed by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment.

Domains of Expertise

Research Domains

Aeroelasticity

Analyzing structural responses under heavy aerodynamic loading forces. Modeling dynamic lifting instabilities, flutter properties, divergence thresholds, and fluid-structure interaction profiles using computational strategies.

Structural Dynamics

Investigating continuous vibrating characteristics across complex structural bodies. Engineering robust mechanisms for dynamic suppression, active damping loops, harmonic vibration responses, and transient phenomena mitigation.

Composite Structures

Evaluating multi-layered anisotropic composite shell topologies. Optimizing specialized configuration matrices for lightweight structures subjected to directional loading conditions and extreme environmental variants.

Finite Element Methods

Formulating extensive spatial approximations across structural systems. Developing non-linear elastic-plastic mesh models, boundary condition analyses, and rigorous convergence profiles using modern solvers.

Machine Learning / AI

Applying data-driven neural network frameworks, surrogate parameter analytics, and automated feature selection pipelines to discover advanced materials and track health indicators across physical mechanical components.

MATLAB & Control Systems

Designing sophisticated physical feedback mechanisms, PID tuning operations, classical root-locus architectures, state-space representations, and non-linear system linearization scripts via Simulink environments.
