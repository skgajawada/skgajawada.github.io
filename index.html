Academic Projects

A study on the effect of elastic-plastic material properties of metals on hardness values using finite element analysis

The objective of this project is to analyze how the elastic-plastic material properties of metals on hardness values vary from theoretical models to experimental data, and the subsequent optimization of these hardness values utilizing Finite Element Analysis frameworks.

Study of internal combustion engines & their components

Introduced baseline mechanics of Internal Combustion Engines and their subcomponents. Traced operational kinematics and structural mechanisms of variant categories, prioritizing comparative analysis between two-stroke and four-stroke thermal engines.

Hydraulic Robotic Arm for pick and place operation

Designed and configured a custom operational physical prototype to master structural fluid mechanics. Implemented fundamental hydrostatic force transmissions to mechanical joints executing automated tracking movements based explicitly upon Pascal's Law.
