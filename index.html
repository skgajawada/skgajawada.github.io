About Me

Researcher, Educator and Lifelong Learner

I am a PhD Research Scholar in Mechanical Engineering (Machine Design) at the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad. My research focuses on Machine Design, Structural Dynamics, Flutter Analysis, Finite Element Analysis, Composite Structures and Computational Mechanics.

My work integrates advanced numerical modelling with machine learning and digital twin technologies to develop intelligent engineering solutions for aerospace and mechanical systems.

With more than seven years of teaching experience, I have taught several undergraduate mechanical engineering subjects while actively participating in research, faculty development programmes and academic collaborations.

Research

Research Areas

My research integrates classical mechanical engineering with modern computational techniques to improve the analysis, design and intelligent control of engineering systems.

Machine Design

Design and analysis of engineering components considering strength, stiffness, reliability and manufacturability.

Finite Element Analysis

Numerical modelling of mechanical and aerospace structures using advanced finite element techniques.

Structural Dynamics

Dynamic behaviour, vibration analysis, modal analysis and active vibration suppression of engineering systems.

Flutter Analysis

Computational aeroelasticity, flutter prediction and active flutter control for aerospace structures.

Composite Structures

Mechanical behaviour, modelling and optimization of laminated and sandwich composite structures.

AI & Machine Learning

Data-driven modelling, intelligent diagnostics, predictive analytics and digital twin technologies.
